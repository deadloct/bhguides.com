Vicious' T19 D4 Sele's Brute Force Method

For those who are as lazy as I am and don't want to bother with complicated strategies or invest too much into a build — or simply don't have Protoplague — this "strategy" (I put it in quotation marks because it can hardly be called one) might be useful.

Build

Gear

  • Stats: ~100k
  • Full T21 set with T21 Leonidas Fangs mainhand
  • T20 Clover
  • Pets are disabled in this dungeon

Runes

  • 3x Empower
  • 1x Dual Strike
  • 1x Kor (extra survivability)
  • 1x Othale (extra Empower)
  • 1x Boli (helps finish off bosses)

Enchants

  • 6x Empower

Familiars

Teithyus (x2)

  • Pumps: 3x evade
  • Chip: damage reduction
  • Bone: redirection (any bone works)
  • Brains are disabled in this dungeon

Strategy

The formation consists of 2 Teithyus and Vicious in the front row. The Teithyus are there purely to provide survivability and prevent regular mobs from killing you—virtually all the damage comes from Sele's 4/4 or the 2 SP and 3 SP skills.

The strategy is simply to get lucky with the bosses. I didn't notice any meaningful difference in difficulty between them. If you're lucky, they'll die from attacking you. It took me around 20 attempts to clear D4, since the common mobs can kill you after you've killed the 3 bosses if you're unlucky and Sele's 4/4 doesn't proc. When that happens, use the 3 SP or 2 SP skill to finish them off.

Empower is essential—you need to hit the bosses hard and kill them as quickly as possible.
