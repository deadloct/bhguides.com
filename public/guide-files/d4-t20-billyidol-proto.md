# BillyIdol's Budget Proto Guide

I recently completed this dungeon on my alt ChillyIdol, which doesn't have all the gear and fams that my main has. Proto is just that strong where it doesn't matter. While the build below could use much more dual strike, I don't have that luxury on this alt.

> Note: This guide is the same as my t19d4 proto guide, with the exception that the pet and fam brains actually do something in this d4, and I didn't need to reforge gear within the dungeon.

## Build

* _Set_: Proto 4/4
* _Offhand_: Pressure Shield
* _Neck_: t18 Clover
* _Accessory_: Ascendancy +4 (would have been better to use an accessory with more DS, even an epic or leg)
* _Pet_: Kolg +1
* _Mount_: Leg Peach +2. Stats: 0/2528/8965, 3.75% evade.
* _Runes_:
    * Major: 4x dual strike (2x leg and 2x myth)
    * Up Triangle: Epic bolster
    * Down Triangle: Leg dual strike
    * Meta: Mythic overhealing to shield meta (theory being that proto heartwood is a heal that triggers this)
* _Enchants_: 3x myth empower, 3x myth accuracy/speed split enchants (don't judge me, the NFT already had these 3 enchants when I got it 🤣)
* _Stats_: 10938/7618/81500, Total: 100056. I entered with 118501 though.

## Fams

4x Googie Moogienz with the following augments:
* _Pumps_: 3x leg block (totaling 70.8% block)
* _Chip_: Leg block chip (14.4% block)
* _Bone_: Epic redirect bone (18% redirect)
* _Brain_: Myth attack team when hit brain

## Strategy

I spammed 0sp. SP resets every battle so you rarely get a chance to do much else.

The hope is that you connect on a dual strike chain and eliminate everybody in one skill use.
