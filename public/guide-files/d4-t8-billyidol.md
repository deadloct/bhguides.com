# BillyIdol's In-Tier Harvester Guide

Perhaps the only useful things in this guide are my stat spread and enemy kill order. Having Zarli as a pet and Kris Gemmgle as my DPS made this one very easy.

## Build

* Total stats: 3308
* Stat spread: 568/808/1932
* Set: Inferno
* Mainhand: Exodus
* Ring: Engulfing Artifact
* Accessory: Jupingz +3
* Pet: Zarli +1
* Mount: Peach +4, 4/30/339 spread, 3.75% life steal

Runes:
* Majors: 1x major speed (legendary), 3x major dual strike (2x mythic, 1x legendary)
* Up Triangle: Kofra (epic -- 3% bolster)
* Down Triangle: Thork (epic -- while at full health, gain 6% dual strike)
* Meta: Erlun (legendary -- 20% max shields)

Enchants:
* 2x empower (mythic)
* 4x various enchants (legendary)

## Familiars

Hoplag +5:
* Pumps: 3x evade (1 legendary, 2 rare)
* Chip: evade (epic)
* Bone: redirect (epic)
* Brain: heal team when you get hit (mythic)

Kris Gemmgle +5:
* Pumps: 1x ricochet (legendary), 1x dual strike (rare), 1x empower (rare)
* Chip: bolster (legendary)
* Bone: overkill damage ricochets (rare)
* Brain: attack random enemy when you hit an enemy (epic)

## Strategy

Kill order: Brimby/Gooz/Cramlich, then Vicky, then others.

The team and build can auto some parts of the dungeon (see video), but facing a mob
of 5 rough enemies was always a manual challenge.

Kris Gemmgle's healing is fantastic for a DPS, and his freeze is likely beneficial as well.

I mostly spammed Exodus' 2sp "Deals x damage to the weakest enemy, 3 times" to kill the hard DPS familiars, then it didn't matter too much if I did 2sp or the 3sp that hits a random enemy 7x.
