# Sora: What to do when you're stuck

> *"I am stuck!"*
> *"What do I do?"*

## Is my gear/mount fully upgraded?

- **No?** Then work on that — Total Stat matters.
- **Yes?** Move to the next question.

## Am I using the right runes/enchants?

- **No?** Then work on that.
- **Yes?** Proceed to the next question.

## Am I using the right pet/accessories?

- **Using guild pet/acc?** Replace them with a real pet/acc from the shop or leaderboards.
- **Pet/acc not suited for the build?** (e.g. Def acc on a DPS build) Try to get something decent/useful at least.
- **Yes?** Move to the next question.

## Am I using the right familiars?

- **No?** Then work on that.
- **Yes?** Move to the next question.

## Are my familiars fully stabled?

- **No?** Then work on that.
- **Yes?** Move to the next question.

## Did I equip the right augments (or any augments at all) on my familiars?

- **No?** Then work on that. Just craft 🟦 Rare augments — don't make 🟧 Epic augments, and save for 🟨 Legendaries.
- **Yes?** Move to the next question.

## Do I need to turn off manual when fighting boss/roadblock flags?

- **Yes** — go manual, learn the enemy's skills, and decide what to do based on that.
- **No** — if you're way overgeared for it.
