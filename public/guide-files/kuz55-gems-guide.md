# Early Game Gem Use / Moderate Spending

## RS packs

Most players buy RS packs (baskets/rolls/pouches/plaques) for materials and pulls.

## What endgame players chase

Boost boxes (e.g., Ultra Lucky Boost Box) to try for ultra boosts (Ultragor 1200%). Used to grind world bosses for long sessions (~12 hours) to maximize legendary, set, and mythic drops.

## Brains / augments

Early game: brains aren’t crucial — epics will carry you far.

**Endgame Brains**: Mythic Attack “Weakest on Hit” and Mythic “When Hitting.” Direct-craft these when possible rather than relying on pure RNG.

**Pumps to prioritize**: Speed, Damage Reduction, Barrier, DS/Emp, Deflect.

## Crafting & materials

Craft legendary pumps, reroll for good stats, and scrap bad ones to gather Gloomsteel and other mats.

Roll eggs/chests to obtain legendary accessories and legendary pets so you can craft mythics.

Never scrap a pet or accessory until it’s been upgraded 3 times — that yields the most legendary materials for mythic crafting.

## Gems & spending

You can spend gems to persuade legendary familiars if gold bribes fail, but don’t do this early on.

Best moderate real-money value: $20 packs (10 packs + 2k gems).

Best big spend value: Ultra Push Pack.

Mid-Game materials boost: 4,500-gem Large Bit-o-Mat barrel is a solid buy if you are impatient.

## Strategy tips

Save gems early. Focus on placing high in weekly rewards to earn crafting materials.

Many players camp lower tiers to rank higher in weekly rewards and get more materials while spending less currency.

You don’t need to buy augment packs to get a usable legendary brain — patience, crafting, and weekly farming are sufficient.
