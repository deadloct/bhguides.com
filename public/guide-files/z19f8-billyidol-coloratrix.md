# ColoraTrix

## Build

I used a fairly standard DPS build on this one: T18 4/4 Aeneas, T19 2/4 Makridi (for the bolster), a +4 Dracus, +2 Druffo, and a +0 Shokan Attachment. I did not record it at the time, but I believe I had a mix of DS and Empower runes and enchants.

## Fams

GlarzDos: 2x +5 with a myth shield-team brain, 1 leg/2 epic DR pumps, a leg redirect bone, and a leg DR-while-team-alive chip.

Ukalis: 1x +5 with a myth attack-strongest-enemy brain, leg Empower pumps, a leg bonus-dmg chip, and an epic SP regen bone.

## Strategy

I did not have a tank at the time, but it would be better to put a tank in the back because ColoraTrix's 0sp hits the furthest target.

Aeneas shocks the furthest target as a passive, which is beneficial with ColoraTrix in the back. The 0sp shocks the strongest target, which may or may not hit the boss, but the 2sp definitely helps by shocking the furthest target.

Other than that, don't waste super pots like I did unless absolutely necessary.
