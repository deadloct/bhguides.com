# The Gooz

## Build

I wore 3x t5 set pieces and 3x t7 epics. Mount was a legendary +1, and I had an epic DPS acc and leg Nelson pet.

## Fams

In this video I used a +0 xX_DarkOverlord_Xx and a +0 Mir'alg. Given the lack of stabling, my augments are irrelevant.

## Strategy

The Gooz hits the front target on 0sp and a random target on 1sp. If you time it right, you can predict when he will hit front, helping you plan your skills and swap positions if needed.

Mir'alg is great for pushing flags. My TS was low due to the t5 set gear and my DarkOverlord was only +0 (fusing more requires being in a higher tier), but Mir'alg's "How It's Done" protection skill caused the DarkOverlord to heal for 1186 on The Gooz's next hit instead of taking the damage at 0:26.

If you're struggling with The Gooz, try Mir'alg. It's available from the Festiviflux Invasion.
