# King Brimby

## Build

I wore 3x t5 set pieces and 3x t7 epics. Mount was a legendary +1, and I had an epic DPS acc and leg Nelson pet.

## Fams

In this video I used a +0 xX_DarkOverlord_Xx and a +0 Mir'alg. Given the lack of stabling, my augments are irrelevant.

## Strategy

King Brimby hits the closest target with 0sp, the furthest target with 1sp, and the selected target with 2sp (which defaults to the weakest target on auto). The first two skills make his hits somewhat predictable, so you can try to move your tank around as he fills up SP.

Mir'alg is a great familiar for pushing flags. My TS was low due to the t5 set gear, and my DarkOverlord was only +0 (not a high enough tier to fuse more), but Mir'alg's "How It's Done" protection skill caused the DarkOverlord to heal in critical moments, like at 0:19.

I misplayed the skill later, not predicting how Brimby would attack very well, but I still managed to beat him pretty easily.

If you're struggling with Brimby, try Mir'alg. It's available from the Festiviflux Invasion.
